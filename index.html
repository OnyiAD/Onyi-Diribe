Travel to Dubai

Dubai in the UAE is a must-visit destination for any traveller, tourist, or businessman. From exotic resorts, theme parks and hotels to downtown city living, from luxury restaurants and malls to street food and open markets, and from sandboarding to surfing and skydiving, the city offers lots to see and do. Here are three of the sights and sounds of Dubai that are a must-see for every visitor:

Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall and the Musical Dancing fountains

Once known as the world's tallest building, the Burj Khalifa is an exquisite work of architecture. Famous for its luxury and expledent firework displays, this building will take your breath away.


Source: Wikipedia

The Dubai mall is home to a variety of top brand shops in jewelry, clothing and accessories and many more. This mall is one of the largest in the world and offers a range of activities including:

  • Shopping
  • Restaurant dining,
  • Aquarium viewing,
  • Ice skating, and so much more.

    • The dancing fountains are located just outside the mall and can be viewed every 30 minutes from 7pm. These musical marvels are displayed to dance to famous classical tunes.

    These three places can be easily accessed using the Dubai metro and explored side by side to create a wholesome experience.
